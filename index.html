I’m running for Oklahoma House District 86 because rural communities deserve a representative who shows up, listens, and protects what makes this place worth calling home.

Congress should look like us.

I’m an average American. I work hard for what I have—just like the folks of District 86. I don’t have all the answers, but I do have values: human decency, common sense, and justice in our government.
